Our Mission

At the Elderly Feeding Program, our mission is to ensure that every senior citizen aged 75 in kitui central and Kilungu,Makueni county have access to nutritious meals and comprehensive care. We believe that good nutrition and home care packages is essential for maintaining the health and well-being of our elderly population.

Through our meal delivery service, community meals, and personalized nutrition advice and care packages we strive to improve the quality of life for seniors and help them live independently for as long as possible.

About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of elderly individuals in our community. Our mission is to provide comprehensive care and support to seniors, ensuring they have access to nutritious meals, and personalized nutrition advice.

Our team of passionate volunteers and experienced professionals work tirelessly to deliver meals directly to the homes of seniors who may have difficulty preparing their own food. We also organize community meals, where seniors can come together, enjoy a shared meal, and engage in social interaction.
